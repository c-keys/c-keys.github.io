naKey what?

Pronounced nah-key. This keyboard is best enjoyed raw without a case. Designed for its PCB aesthetic and its ability to more easily introduce the components on a printed circuit board. The only surface mounted part is the Atmega32u4 (if you take one of our workshops, this chip will come pre-soldered). All other components are through-hole. We chose to do this for aesthetics (through-hole so pretty), beginner-friendly soldering, and easier component identification.

Bill of Materials

  • naKey PCB
  • Atmega32u4 (pre-soldered if from cKeys)
  • Mini USB port
  • 16 MHz Crystal
  • Reset Switch
  • Diodes x 17
  • 22 Ohm Resistors x 2
  • 10k Ohm Resistors x 2
  • 1 uf Capacitors x 2
  • 27uF Crystal Capacitors x 2
  • 0.1uF Decoupling Capacitors x 4
  • Rubber Bumpons x 4
