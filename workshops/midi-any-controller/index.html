Build your own MIDI controller!

Now with the new Proton C ARM based microcontroller!

Would you like to make digital sound and music with (almost) anything? This is your chance to build a midi controller that will allow you to solder or alligator clip wires to real world objects of your choosing. Our kit is heavily inspired by Makey Makey but with a built in piezo speaker (for that chiptune vibe), a midi first focus, and programmability via QMK.

In this workshop you will solder our MIDI Any controller which includes a PCB, a Proton C microcontroller, and alligator clip wires. This is workshop is suitable for all soldering skill levels.
